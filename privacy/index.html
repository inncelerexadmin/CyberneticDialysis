Privacy policy

How we handle personal data on this website.

Summary

This website does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

We use essential local storage to remember dismissal of the cookie notice.

Controller and contact

Data controller: Cybernetic Dialysis.

Contact: contact@cyberneticdialysis.com.

What data we process

We may process basic technical data needed to serve the site (e.g. browser/device information in standard server logs).

We store a single preference in your browser to remember that you dismissed the cookie notice.

If you contact us via the enquiry form, we process the information you submit (name, position, company, email, phone number, and enquiry message).

Cookies / local storage

Essential storage: we store a simple “dismissed” flag for the cookie notice in localStorage.

No analytics cookies: we do not run analytics at this time.

No advertising cookies: we do not run ad tracking.

Lawful basis

Essential site storage and operational processing are handled on the basis of legitimate interests and/or necessity for providing the website.

Contact form processing is handled on the basis of legitimate interests (responding to enquiries) and/or taking steps at your request prior to entering into a contract.

Retention

Cookie notice preference stays in your browser until you clear site data.

Server logs (if any) are retained for operational and security purposes according to hosting provider configuration.

Enquiry submissions are delivered via Formspree and retained according to our mailbox retention and Formspree settings.

Your rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise rights, contact contact@cyberneticdialysis.com.

Contact forms

If you submit an enquiry via our contact form, we process the information you provide (name, position, company, email, phone number, and enquiry message) to respond to your request.

Form submissions are handled by Formspree, which delivers your message to our email inbox. Formspree may process technical information (such as IP address and user agent) for delivery and abuse prevention. We do not use analytics or advertising trackers.

Formspree privacy policy: https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.
